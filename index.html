InfoTown

Manifesto

We believe every organisation has a hidden town.

A place where information is created, trusted, moved and used.

Most people work there every day... ...they just haven't seen it yet.

InfoTown exists to make this hidden world visible and accessible to everyone.

Not through standards.

Not through jargon.

But through a new kind of language.

A language of stories, places and people.

One that explains the complex, simplifies the challenging and brings clarity to the confusing.

Because information becomes knowledge.

Knowledge becomes understanding.

Understanding changes the world.

Everything is connected.

Everyone is welcome.

Welcome to InfoTown.

InfoTown is under contruction.
